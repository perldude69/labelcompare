        How to use LabelCompare

        Goal: Quickly check that the label on the bottle matches what the company wrote on the official form.

        Simple steps

        1. Add files
          Drag PDFs into the left list or click Upload PDF.
        2. Check them
          Click a file, then Analyze. Or click Batch Analyze to do several at once.
          You’ll see a clear “Looks good” or “Review needed” at the top.
        3. Look at the key checks
          The table shows what the form says vs. what’s actually printed on the label.
          Pay special attention to the government warning box — it must be the exact text and in ALL CAPS.
        4. Decide
          After analysis, the file is automatically sorted into the Passed or Failed folder based on the 3 requirements (All CAPS government warning, brand match to the form, and ABV match).
          Batch mode does this for all files at once.

        Tips

        • Always open “See what the AI read” if something looks wrong — you can compare it to the actual PDF on the right.
        • Click Analyze again anytime to re-check a file.
        • The tool is here to help catch obvious problems fast. You still make the final call.
        • Drag the lines between the three columns to give the picture more or less room.
        • Reset (top right) clears everything if you want to start over.

        Everything runs on your computer. Nothing is sent anywhere.
